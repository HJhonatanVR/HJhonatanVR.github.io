Five hectares, 12 acres, of tropical gardens in the cloud forest of the Machu Picchu Historical Reserve, one of the New 7 Wonders of the World. Guests can visit the garden of native orchids in their natural habitat – 372 species, a world record according to the American Orchid Society, the organic Tea Plantation which provide daily the hotel with their organic products, Andean Spectacle Bear Rescue project. Member of Virtuoso; EcoLuxury; verified by Rainforest Alliance for its rigorous sustainability standards; listed in Travel+Leisure 500 World’s Best Hotel 2010-2011-2012-2013; T+L Awards for The World`s Best Hotels for Families 2011; Condé Nast Traveller’s Gold List 2010-2012-2013. Since 2013, Inkaterra Machu Picchu Suites are member of Relais & Châteaux.



   

A private high-end authentic lodge and ecological reserve located at the edge of Tambopata Reserve in the Amazon rainforest of Southern Peru, one of the New 7 Natural Wonders of the World. It offers a selection of à-la-carte excursions within the reserve and its surroundings, among which the spectacular 344m (1,129ft) long and 30m (100ft) high Canopy Walkway, which allows an incredible experience from over the tree tops; even more incredible is an overnight stay in our Canopy Tree House, a cozy room perched at 30m (100ft) above the ground, that allows to feel nature from a unique point of view. Member of EcoLuxury; verified by Rainforest Alliance for its sustainable practice; and listed in the National Geographic Traveler’s Stay List 2011 and 2012.



   

First Relais & Châteaux hotel in Peru is an exquisite 16th century colonial mansion, located in the heart of the historic Inca capital of Cusco. Carefully restored to retain its historical heritage, it presents modern luxury without sacrificing authenticity, capturing the sense of privacy and privilege. Andrew Harper Alliance Property, member of XO Private Collection, and EcoLuxury. Listed in the Condé Nast Traveler’s US “Hot List 2009”; Condé Nast Traveller’s UK “Gold List 2011” in the Ambiance and Design Category; T+L The World’s Greatest Hotels and Resorts + Spas 2010; and the Environment Trophy 2013 by Relais & Châteaux.
