    Mural "Llajtapuñuy"

    Please read the brief History about the mural..

    he sun god seeing the corruption of his town "the titikaka" decided to punish and submerge it. To fulfill his plan, he sends his son "Manco Capac" and his wife "Mama Ocllo", they came out of the lake (puma stone) to create a new empire. They taught different activities to the people of there as the working of the land And the harvest of the potato, quinua, barley and corn, Products that are made in the area "Altiplanica". The new people of indomitable race have a feature on the main that is pleasing to the deities as the "Pacha Mama" and the sun god With the body language. The three deities the condor (World of top), the puma (Worl of now) and the snake (Worl below), are What the God of the sun sends for that man does not feel forsaken. Puno with its variety of dances, in vast majority carnivals cheerful, dedicated as a thank you to mother earth "the Pachamama" to give their agricultural products of the earth. Finally the "gang Puneña" (Festive dance) is a dance of young love where the cholita falls in love with the man coming down from the hill in a loud voice saying enough, enough my love you already have another love. All these things in gratitude to the "Mamacha Candelaria" mother of Puno.
